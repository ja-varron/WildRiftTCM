## **BUB_TC_001:** Single Bubble - Shaded

> **Summary:** This test case verifies the ability of the app to detect a single shaded answer bubble (e.g., for "A") without any stray marks.

**Preconditions:** _Valid scanned image_

Scenario 1

| # | Steps | Expected Behavior |
|-----|-----|-----|
| 1 | Scan paper with one bubble shaded per question. | The system should detect all the bubbles and display the correct marks for each question. |
